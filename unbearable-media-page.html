UNBEARABLE LIGHTNESS OF MEDIA

Newspaper, Plastic bucket
2 x 2.5 (m)
Independent work, 2019 Summer

When it comes to reading news, people usually depend on its frame rather than its context. The frame—such as the authority and the name value of the media—shapes our judgement and leads us to accept information ignorantly. Thus, if the newspapers were to get “cleaned”, only the frame of the media mechanism will be left. The most important and the most valuable things are usually too weighty and fall down, where they remain invisible to people.
