A CHAIR OF BELIEF

Plastic bandage, Steel mesh, Marker
10 x 30 (cm)
Independent work, 2019 Summer

Sometimes, or most of the time, we debate over the existence such as whether the fate exists; whether the past life exists; or whether the alien exists. People go through various scientific researches and experiments to prove those, but “a belief in existence itself” is the most persuasive evidence over any other scientific backgrounds. By creating a chair for alien, regardless of the truth, I successfully prove the existence of alien that no one could do before. It is not important whether alien is real or not, but there is another sphere of thought beyond the boundary that we recognize.
