HUMAN PUPA

Acrylic paint, Pen, Iron net, Hair
1 x 0.69 x 1 (m)
Independent work, 2019 Summer

As silkworm produces their shelter with their own resources, human also should create self-producing house with available material in their own body. This work demonstrates that pupa made of human hair is the most intrinsic space for us since it is created with our own resources and it does not cause any excessive waste of natural resources and environment pollution.
