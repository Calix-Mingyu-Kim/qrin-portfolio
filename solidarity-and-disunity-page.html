SOLIDARITY AND DISUNITY

Acrylic painting
393 x 545 (mm)
Independent work, 2019 Summer

The relationship is somehow bound as like spiderweb, but it is also somehow split as like broken glass. There is only fine line between solidarity and disunity. As like two painting pieces look similar, relationship depends on how we look at it and how we regard it.
