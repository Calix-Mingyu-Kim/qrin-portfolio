AN ORDINARY DINNER

Various mediums
1.5 x 2.0 (m)
Independent work, 2019 Summer

Every furniture has an attribute which is “function” accords with its definition of supporting our lives. But it is superficial. The function is not given by the creator, but by the user. Perhaps, the compulsion to use furniture only for its intended purpose constrains our lives. This work might looks ridiculous on the outside, but a person who are inside of the picture totally feel comfortable and familiar.
